## 6. Guidelines

### Do
- Use 4px grid for all spacing
- Use `#FF3C00` for primary actions and CTAs
- Stick to 4 font weights: 400, 500, 600, 700
- Use `ABC Diatype Plus` as the primary typeface

### Don't
- Don't introduce colors outside the palette above
- Don't mix fonts beyond ABC Diatype Plus and Inter
- Don't use inline styles when the design system covers the pattern
- Don't use border-radius values outside: 0px 12px 12px 0px, 6px, 8px, 12px, 12px 0px 0px 12px, 16px, 50%, 9999px
